Nadi's Hobbies!

Hello there! Here are some things that I really enjoy and why :)

photography


Photography was a hobby I picked up as
soon I was gifted a point-and-shoot when
I was in second grade. As a heavy sentimentalist,
I was fascinated how a camera allowed me to
document these memories wherever I go.

I grew to love the art of creating new perspectives
of everyday’s subjects and motions. I also love
being a part of people’s special moments, and
giving them something to remember them by. I now
do shoots for weddings, portraits, and lifestyle.


Costa Rica, 2022. Two kids playing on the beach.

music


Music is a shared passion within my family.
I grew up playing the piano, and loving whatever
classical or rock music my dad would listen to. As someone that
Is often at a “loss for words”, I find music both an interesting and
beautiful form of communication.
I picked up the guitar and ukulele later, and I enjoy plaguing
my house with my singing. I love listening to music,
and I enjoy jamming with my brother often.


My house, 2022. A friend during a jam session.

travel


I have a lot of family outside of the country, so my family travelled often.
While we were away, we would explore neighboring destinations where
I developed my love for immersing myself in cultures different than mine,
understanding new lifestyles, and meeting new people.

Alaska, 2021. My brother on a train.
